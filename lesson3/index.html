Site Purpose

The purpose of The Hiking Guide is to help individuals or groups prepare for their hikes by knowing what the weather will be that day, information about individual hikes, and what they might need to bring. The goal of The Hiking Guide is to be a community of hikers that can put out information to eachother about hikes they've been on while also being supplied enough information from the host for them to be fully prepared for their hike.
