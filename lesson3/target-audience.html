Target Audience

Who is The Hiking Guide's Target Audience?

Age: 25-70

Income: $30k-80k

Education-Level: High School Graduate Degree, Undergraduate Degree

Location: United States of America


Has past experience with hiking and goes hiking at least twice a month. Visitor's will mainly be accessing this site from their homes on most likely their mobile phones or laptops. The majority will visit this site the day before a hike or the morning of.

Scenarios

  • I want to go hiking tomorrow, but I'm not sure where I can go.
  • I wonder what the weather will be like for my hike on Thursday.
  • What would I need to bring with me on this hike?
  • How long is this hike going to be?
  • Is the sun going to go down before I finish my hike?
